10 Places To Brunch At

Cafedral

Located near the Durham Cathedral, Cafedral is a quaint, cozy caf√© with a good variety of breakfast, lunch, and tea choices.

Address: Owengate, Durham DH1 3HB
Opening Hours: 10am - 5.30pm daily, closed on Monday

Chapters Tearooms

A popular spot with students, locals and tourists alike, Chapters Tea Rooms serves up delicious savoury and sweet treats.

Address: 16 Elvet Bridge, Durham DH1 3AA
Opening Hours: 9am - 5.30pm on Mon-Sat, 10am - 5pm on Sunday

Ciao Ciao

A Mediterranean deli serving up authentic Greek and Italian cuisine. Everything is prepared freshly in-house and you can treat yourself to some artisanal breads from their on-site bakery.

Address: 3 Silver St, Durham DH1 4SJ
Opening Hours: 8am - 6pm on Mon-Sat, 10am - 5pm on Sunday

Flat White Kitchen

Serving excellent brunch fare and one of the best coffees in town, Flat White Kitchen has taken Durham by storm since its opening in 2015. Be prepared to queue at this hugely popular cafe, or head over to its sister establishment just round the corner, Flat White Cafe, serving drinks and light snacks.

Address: 40 Saddler St, Durham DH1 3NU
Opening Hours: 9am - 4pm on Tues-Sat, 10am - 4pm on Sunday, closed on Monday
(It is also open for dinner on Thurs-Sat from 6-10pm)

Leonard's Coffee House

Located in the city centre but hidden just off the main streets, Leonard's is a popular spot with locals and students alike. Serving an exclusive coffee blend and excellent range of savoury and sweet dishes, Leonard's is a great place if you are looking for good quality food and coffee.

Address: 1 Back Silver St, Fowler's Yard, Durham DH1 3RA
Opening Hours: 8.30am - 5pm on Mon-Sat, 9am - 5pm on Sun

The Pancake Cafe @ The Georgian Townhouse

You are bound to find what you like in this lovely little cafe with a delicious range of sweet and savoury pancakes. With wonderful service and a homely touch, The Pancake Cafe is a great place for bringing your family, friends or date.

Address: 11 Crossgate, Durham DH1 4PS
Opening Hours: 10am - 5pm on Tues-Sat, 11am - 3pm on Sun, closed on Monday

Treats Tea Room

Good old-fashioned food with friendly service at the heart of Durham city centre. It tends to get crowded fast so get there early!

Address: 27-28 Silver St, Durham DH1 3RD
Opening Hours: 8.30am - 5.30pm on Mon-Fri, 8.30am - 6pm on Sat, 9.30 - 4.30pm on Sun

Vennels Cafe

Award-winning cafe with a wonderful historic ambience that is especially popular with locals. Vennels prides itself on delivering homemade traditional English fare of a high quality.

Address: Saddlers Yard, 71 Saddler St, Durham DH1 3NP
Opening Hours: 9.30am - 5.30pm on Mon-Sat, 10.30am - 5.30pm on Sun

Waffley Good Company

The most iconic waffle spot in town dishing out both sweet and savoury variations of the beloved dish. Enjoy some good company while you tuck in to some delicious waffles.

Address: 95 Elvet Bridge, Durham DH1 3AG
Opening Hours: 10am - 4.30pm daily, closed on Tuesday

9 Altars Cafe

Tucked away off Silver Street, this cosy little cafe serves a wonderful variety of homemade treats for breakfast, lunch or tea. Check out their great value deals and chill with your friends in their lovely outdoor sitting area when the weather is good.

Address: 19A Silver St, Durham DH1 3RB
Opening Hours: 9.30am - 5.30pm daily
